## Short description (80 chars max)

## Full description (4000 chars max)
LifeFlow Bridge connects your Life Network subscription to any podcast player on your Android device.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Sign in with your Life Network email and password
2. The app starts a local feed server on your phone
3. Tap Subscribe on any podcast to add it to your podcast player (Podcast Addict, AntennaPod, etc.)
4. Episodes stream directly from Life Network's servers

FEATURES
- Standard RSS feeds compatible with any podcast app
- Automatic feed and auth token refresh every 30 minutes
- Foreground service keeps the server running while you listen
- Secure credential storage using Android's encrypted keystore
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics

REQUIREMENTS
- An active Life Network subscription (app.joinlifenetwork.com)
- An Android podcast player that supports RSS feeds

PRIVACY
- Credentials are stored locally on your device, encrypted
- The feed server runs on localhost only — not accessible from the network
- No data is collected or sent anywhere except to Life Network's API
- Full privacy policy: https://100110010111.github.io/lifeflow/privacy-policy.html

Open source: https://github.com/100110010111/lifeflow
